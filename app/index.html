HOWL.js

HOWL.js is an audio protocol for sharing HTML 5 games. It allows players to join each others' games and share game information.

Why sound?

  • All mobile devices have microphones and speakers
  • Power consumption - only generate sound when needed
  • Promote local play - local multiplayer
